Welcome to Sunshine House, your home for authentic Cantonese specialties delivered with flair. From traditional favorites to innovative delights, savor the flavors of China in every bite.

Delicious Cantonese

Embark on a gastronomic journey through the heart of Canton with our exquisite Cantonese-inspired cuisine at Sunshine House. From the bustling markets of Guangzhou to the tranquil tea houses of Hong Kong, our menu pays homage to the rich tapestry of flavors and culinary traditions of the region. Savor the symphony of flavors as you indulge in our signature dishes, meticulously crafted to capture the essence of Cantonese cooking. From succulent roasted meats and crispy fried delights to steaming bowls of comforting noodles and delicate dim sum, each bite is a celebration of freshness, quality ingredients, and time-honored techniques.

At Sunshine House, we're committed to delivering an authentic Cantonese dining experience that transcends borders. Whether you're craving the comforting warmth of a hearty bowl of congee or the bold flavors of a spicy Szechuan stir-fry, our diverse menu offers something to tantalize every palate.

Join us as we pay homage to the culinary heritage of Canton, where every dish tells a story of tradition, innovation, and the vibrant spirit of the region. Come experience the flavors of Cantonese cuisine like never before at Sunshine House – your passport to an unforgettable dining adventure.
